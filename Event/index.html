DAVID BOWIE

THE LEGACY TOUR

Paramount Theatre 911 Pine St, Seattle, WA 98101

Join us at the Paramount Theatre in Seattle to celebrate the music and legacy of David Bowie. This great chameleon artist touched many hearts throughout his life, so this is our thanks to him. Featuring the talents of Weezer, Adam Lambert, Ninja Sex Party, and many local bands. After the show, relax and rejoice at the MoPOP with a special screening of Labyrinth.
