Greetings, Earthling.

I’m Cactus Springman, a freelance illustrator and character artist who landed in Seattle, WA about 22 Earth years ago.

I partake in many human cultures such as video gaming, watching cartoons, and petting small feline creatures.

I specialize in digital illustration, but my talents are flexible. If you’re searching for quality art depicting monsters, furries, wacky characters, or weird animals, then you’ve come to the right alien-er, fellow human.
